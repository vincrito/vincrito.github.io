About Blog

Purpose

This site provides clear, practical information about pancreatic and hepatobiliary diseases, with a focus on how decisions are made in real clinical settings.

It is intended for patients, families, and clinicians seeking a more transparent understanding of diagnosis, treatment options, and surgical decision-making.

What you’ll find here

  • Patient-centered explanations of complex conditions
  • Breakdowns of common clinical decisions
  • Perspectives on uncertainty in surgical oncology
